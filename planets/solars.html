Solar system consists of the Sun and everything orbits it, including planets, dwarf planets, comets, asteroids and much more. Choose below a celestial body for detailed information and visuals.

Let's Go

It's a star

The Sun is the closest star to Earth, it's the source of all the heat and light. Life wouldn't exist without it. It's also the center of our solar system and by far its largest object.
Source: National Geographic

It's alive

Solar system is the only place we know that hosts life. There are 8 planets in the system. Among all those planets, as far as we know, life only exists on Earth.

It's old

Solar System is formed 4.6 billion years ago from a collapsed gas cloud. It took millions of years to shape and cool down all the planets and their moons.
