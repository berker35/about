Mercury

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun but it's not the hottest.

It has a thin atmosphere and no moons.

One Mercurian day is 59 earth days long and a year equals to 88 earth days.

Mercury is the smallest planet.

Mercury is a terrestrial planet and its surface is covered with craters
