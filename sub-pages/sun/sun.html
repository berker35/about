The Sun

The Sun is a star and it's at the center of our Solar System.

The sun is a huge ball of different gases, mostly hydrogen and helium

It emits great amounts of energy which is essential for life on Earth.

There are 8 planets around the sun.

The sun is 4.6 billion years old and 109 times wider than Earth.
