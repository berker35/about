Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun. It is where we live!

The Moon is the Earth's only natural satellite

A terran year is 365 days and a day is 24 hours

The earth is the only planet in the solar system known to possess life.

Earth is a terrestrial planet. Its atmosphere is composed of mostly Nitrogen (78%) and Oxygen (21%). 71% of its surface is covered with water.
